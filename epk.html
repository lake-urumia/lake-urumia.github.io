Lake Urumia

biography

Lake Urumia is an experimental musician and multimedia artist based in Graz, Austria. Working across sound art, video, photography, and text, the practice explores ecological temporalities and the aesthetics of decay through frameworks like "Messy Sensing" – an approach to time-based media that embraces noise, error, and environmental interference as compositional elements.

Over eight years of collaboration with Austrian cultural institutions including Forum Stadtpark, Elevate Festival, and Steirischer Herbst have shaped a body of work that sits at the intersection of drone, noise, and environmental observation. As part of an artist duo focused on transdisciplinary practice, Lake Urumia develops performances, installations, and recordings that investigate the boundaries between signal and noise, presence and absence.

Recent work has focused on urban microclimate monitoring and the sonification of environmental data, drawing connections between ecological sensing and experimental sound practice.
