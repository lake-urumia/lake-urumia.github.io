HOW TO ADD IMAGES TO THE WEBSITE
================================

1. Copy your images into THIS folder (your-images).

2. Rename them, numbered in the order you want them to appear:

      work-1.jpg
      work-2.jpg
      work-3.jpg
      ...

   - No gaps in the numbers: the site stops looking at the first
     missing number.
   - jpg, jpeg, png and webp all work.

3. That's it. A framed "work" gallery appears on the homepage
   automatically, along with a "work" link in the menu.

TIPS
----
- Export images around 1200-1600 px wide and under ~400 KB each
  so the page stays fast on mobile.
- Optional captions: open js/main.js and fill in the WORK_CAPTIONS
  list at the very top of the file (first caption belongs to
  work-1, second to work-2, and so on).
